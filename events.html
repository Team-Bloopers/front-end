Rockshow

The mystic and powerful sound of the Great Highland Bagpipes elevate above the powerful rock band,. influences of Irish Folk, Symphonic Metal, and Classical music merge with the energetic Celtic music, full of lust of life. This sound and the spectacular show guarantee a concert no one will ever forget. Mystic torches on the bagpipes and burning drumsticks, thundering against huge drums, create a cultic atmosphere, flames, shooting out of the instruments, entertain every audience worldwide
